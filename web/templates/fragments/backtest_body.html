{# Genel açıklama — robustness üç bağımsız testi tek çalıştırmada koşar #}
This analysis puts your strategy through three independent tests: Walk-Forward (parameters are optimized in one period and tested in the next, unseen one), In/Out-of-Sample (the data is split in two; the second half is measured as untouched "future") and Monte Carlo (the trade order is shuffled thousands of times to derive the distribution of possible outcomes). Pick a preset or tune the fields by hand.
